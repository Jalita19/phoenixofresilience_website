Who Should Apply

Phoenix of Resilience Foundation welcomes women who are ready to rebuild, heal, and grow. Our transitional housing program is designed for women facing barriers to stability and seeking a supportive path forward.

Women Ages 35 and Up

We focus on empowering mature women ready to start a new chapter, offering personalized support and life planning resources.

Veterans Facing Homelessness

Our program welcomes female veterans who are experiencing housing insecurity, helping them access stability and holistic resources.

Recently Released from Jail or Prison

We provide a safe space for women transitioning from incarceration to rebuild their confidence and independence.

Survivors of Domestic Violence

We offer compassionate housing and emotional support for women healing from abuse and seeking a peaceful, safe environment.

Motivated to Grow

We seek residents who are committed to growth through mentorship, job readiness, and empowerment programs. Please note: we are not accepting applicants with children..

And More

Women facing life-altering challenges — from financial hardship to housing instability — who are ready to commit to personal transformation are encouraged to apply.

Still Unsure If You Qualify?

Our team is here to guide you every step of the way. Whether you have questions about eligibility, housing availability, or next steps — we’re ready to help.
