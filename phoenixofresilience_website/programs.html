Success Stories

Real women. Real resilience. Their journeys inspire the next generation of transformation.

“A Fresh Start”

After completing our Life Skills & Training program, Monica found full-time employment and permanent housing. She now mentors new residents in the program.

“Breaking Barriers”

Tasha joined our Career Development program after years of unemployment. Today she works as a certified peer specialist helping others rebuild.

“Rebuilding Confidence”

Through our Peer Support network, Angela regained her self-confidence and started her own small business providing wellness services to women.
